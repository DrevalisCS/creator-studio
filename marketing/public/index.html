Questions

What hardware do I need?⌃

Minimum: 16 GB RAM, an NVIDIA GPU with ≥ 8 GB VRAM for scenes, ~20 GB disk. For long-form video (Wan 2.2), 24 GB+ VRAM is recommended. No GPU? Use the RunPod integration on Pro/Studio — scenes run on a rented cloud GPU on-demand.

Do I need to install ComfyUI and LM Studio separately?⌃

Yes — both run as external services so you control the models. Our install guide walks through it. Alternatively, wire the app to a RunPod ComfyUI (Pro tier) and Anthropic Claude for scripts — zero local GPU needed.

Can I upgrade or cancel later?⌃

Yes. Visit drevalis.com/account at any time to open Stripe's billing portal — upgrade, downgrade, change payment method, cancel, or view invoices.

What happens if I don't renew?⌃

Your install keeps running for a 7-day grace window, then locks. You can still open the app to view past episodes — generation and upload stop until you renew. Updates are paused.

Is my data sent anywhere?⌃

No. Scripts, voices, videos, and channel credentials stay on your machine. Drevalis servers only handle licensing: a 24-hour heartbeat checks whether your subscription is still active. Nothing about your content is transmitted.
