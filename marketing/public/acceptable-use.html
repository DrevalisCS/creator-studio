Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-21. Part of the Terms of Service.

The Software is a general-purpose content-creation tool. We believe in creator autonomy and we do not read your prompts. But we do require that you not use the Software to produce material that is illegal, harms others, or violates the platforms you publish to. Breaching this policy is grounds for license termination without refund.

You must not use the Software to create, upload, or distribute material that:

  • Sexualizes minors. Any depiction of individuals under 18 in a sexual context is categorically prohibited. This includes photo-real, stylized, animated, or AI-generated depictions. We report credible evidence of such content to law enforcement.
  • Impersonates real people without consent for the purpose of deceiving audiences (deepfake voice or image of a public figure or private person, passed off as authentic). Parody, satire, and commentary that is clearly labeled as such are permitted.
  • Incites violence or hatred against individuals or protected groups on the basis of race, ethnicity, religion, national origin, sexual orientation, gender identity, disability, or similar characteristics.
  • Defames identifiable individuals or businesses by presenting false claims of fact as if true.
  • Promotes self-harm or suicide, facilitates eating disorders, or targets vulnerable audiences with such material.
  • Infringes copyright or trademark rights at scale - e.g. re-voicing a copyrighted audiobook, reconstructing a movie scene-for-scene, mass-producing fan-fiction in a studio's protected style as if licensed.
  • Disseminates disinformation about elections, public-health emergencies, or other topics with a high public-interest stake, especially in a way designed to deceive the audience about authenticity.
  • Facilitates fraud or abuse: phishing scripts, fake testimonials attributed to identifiable people, investment scams, pump-and-dump promos, counterfeit-goods marketing.
  • Violates CSAM, terrorism, or export-control laws in your jurisdiction or in the jurisdiction of your audience.
  • Directly breaks the rules of the upload platform you use - e.g. YouTube's Spam, Deceptive Practices & Scams Policy, or TikTok's Integrity & Authenticity rules on synthetic media.

You must also not:

  • Use the Software's license system in a way it is not designed for - sharing your key, running it on more machines than your tier allows, or tampering with the license-enforcement code;
  • Reverse-engineer, decompile, or extract the Software for the purpose of producing a derivative product;
  • Probe, stress-test, or attempt to exploit our license server or related infrastructure (this is unauthorized access under the Austrian Cybercrime Act / EU Directive 2013/40/EU);
  • Use the Software to send unsolicited bulk messages, scrape platforms beyond their rate limits, or bypass content moderation of the platforms you upload to.

Synthetic-content disclosure

YouTube and TikTok currently require creators to disclose when content is "altered or synthetic" and might be mistaken for a real person, place, or event. We strongly recommend checking the "altered or synthetic content" box (YouTube) or toggling the "AI-generated content" label (TikTok) on any upload that falls in that category. This is your responsibility - the Software does not toggle these flags for you.

Reporting and enforcement

If you believe a user of our Software has published material that violates this policy, email abuse@drevalis.com with the URL and the rule you believe is broken. We do not have access to user installs, so we cannot directly remove content - but we can investigate the license and revoke it if the evidence supports it. For content takedown you must contact the hosting platform.

License revocation for a material breach is immediate and without refund. We will email the affected account with the reason.

Legal content we decline to judge

Outside the categories above, we do not police the topics you cover. Political opinion, adult content for adult audiences (where platform rules permit), strong language, controversial takes - your problem to get right with your audience and platform, not ours.
