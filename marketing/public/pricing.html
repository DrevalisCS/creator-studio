Pricing questions

What's the difference between Free and the live demo?

They're the same thing. Free = the public demo.drevalis.com. Data resets nightly, no real uploads. Use it to decide whether the app is worth paying for.

Why CHF and not USD or EUR?

The business is registered in Switzerland, so CHF is the base currency. Stripe automatically converts to your local currency at checkout — you'll see EUR / USD / GBP on the payment page based on your card.

Can I pay with PayPal?

Yes — PayPal is available on Creator, Pro, and Studio. PayPal bills in CHF and converts at their published rate.

What counts as a "machine"?

A distinct physical or virtual computer running the Drevalis stack, identified by a hashed hostname + MAC address. Reinstalling on the same box does not consume an extra seat.

Can I change tiers mid-subscription?

Yes, from the billing portal. Upgrades are prorated immediately; downgrades take effect at the next renewal.

Refund policy?

14 days, no questions asked — unless the license has been activated on more than one machine or used to publish more than 10 uploads. Email support@drevalis.com.

What happens to my videos if I cancel?

Nothing. Episodes live on your disk. Cancellation gives you a 7-day grace window during which generation and upload still work; after that they lock but existing files are untouched and playable.
