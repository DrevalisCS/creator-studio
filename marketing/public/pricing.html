Pricing questions

What counts as a "machine"?

A distinct physical or virtual computer running the Drevalis Creator Studio stack. Identified by a hashed hostname + MAC address. Reinstalling on the same box does not consume an extra seat. Hit the cap? You can deactivate a machine from Settings → License, or from the activation screen on a new install.

Can I change tiers mid-subscription?

Yes, from the billing portal. Upgrades are prorated immediately; downgrades take effect at the next renewal.

Do I need a GPU?

Strongly recommended — scene generation runs on ComfyUI locally. Minimum ~8 GB VRAM. No GPU? The Pro tier's RunPod integration rents cloud GPUs on-demand (billed separately by RunPod; you bring your own RunPod API key).

Refund policy?

14 days, no questions asked — unless the license has been activated on more than one machine or used to publish more than 10 uploads. Email support@drevalis.com.

What happens to my videos if I cancel?

Nothing. Episodes live on your disk. Cancellation gives you a 7-day grace window during which generation and upload still work; after that they lock but existing files are untouched and playable.

Do you offer team or enterprise plans?

Not yet. Studio with its 5 seats and unlimited channels covers most small teams. For larger deployments or on-prem agreements, email support@drevalis.com and we'll talk.
