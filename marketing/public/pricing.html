FAQ

Do I need a GPU?

Strongly recommended — scene generation runs on ComfyUI locally. Minimum ~8 GB VRAM. No GPU? The Pro tier's RunPod integration rents cloud GPUs on-demand (billed separately by RunPod; you bring your own RunPod API key).

Can I change tiers?

Yes, from the billing portal. Upgrades are prorated immediately; downgrades take effect at the next renewal.

Refund policy?

14 days, no questions asked. Email support@drevalis.com.

What counts as a "machine"?

A distinct physical or virtual computer running the Drevalis Creator Studio stack. A machine is identified by a stable hostname + MAC hash. Reinstalling on the same box does not consume an extra seat.
